The Beginning

Tailoring, for the skin.

The Perfume Tailor began with a simple frustration: fine fragrance felt intimidating and impersonal, beautiful scents sold to everyone, and truly suited to no one. We wanted the opposite: the intimacy of a tailor's fitting room, brought to perfume.

So we gathered the world's most iconic fragrances under one roof, each crafted as a Pure Parfum at 40% oil concentration for unmatched richness and longevity, and made choosing personal again. We listen, we match, we adjust, until you find the one that disappears into you and becomes simply how you smell.
